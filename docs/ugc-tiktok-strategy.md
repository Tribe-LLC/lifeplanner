# Life Planner — TikTok UGC Content System

## Strategic Framework

**Core principle:** Each piece of content = 1 persona × 1 pain scenario × 1 app feature.
Never try to show the whole app. Show one feature that perfectly solves one real problem.

**TikTok structure that converts:**
1. **Hook (0–2s):** Pattern interrupt. Stops the scroll. Never start with "I want to talk about..."
2. **Agitate (2–15s):** Make the pain specific and vivid. The viewer thinks "that's literally me."
3. **Reveal (15–40s):** Show the app solving it. Screen recording, not voiceover.
4. **Proof (40–50s):** Result. Specific number, streak, badge, or feeling.
5. **Soft CTA (50–60s):** Never "download now." Always "link in bio" or "comment X and I'll send it."

## Tier 1 Personas — Start Here

### Persona 1: The Overwhelmed Achiever
**Who:** 22–32, ambitious, drowning in goals they never finish. Tons of notes apps, Notion docs, sticky notes — none connected.

**Pain:** They're not lazy. They're lost. No system. Goals feel abstract.

**App features to show:** Smart Goal Generator → Life Balance wheel → AI Coach

---

#### Hook Pack (Persona 1)

**Hook A** — POV format:
> "POV: you have 47 browser tabs open called 'how to change your life'"

**Hook B** — Confession:
> "I wrote down the same 5 goals every January for 4 years and never finished one"

**Hook C** — Contrast:
> "Other people seem to just... do things. I'd figure out why."

**Hook D** — Question interrupt:
> "What if your goals could actually tell you which one to work on first?"

---

#### Script 1A — "47 tabs"
```
HOOK (0–2s):
[Text overlay: "POV: you have 47 browser tabs open called 'how to change your life'"]
[Show messy desktop or notes app]

AGITATE (2–12s):
"I had goals in my Notes app, goals in Notion, goals on sticky notes — none of them connected.
I'd feel motivated for 3 days, then forget I even had them."

REVEAL (12–40s):
[Screen recording: Open app → type goal description in plain text → AI generates
SMART goal with milestones → see it slot into Life Balance wheel]
"I typed 'I want to get healthy' and it turned it into an actual plan.
With milestones. With a category. With a coach assigned to it."

PROOF (40–52s):
"Three months later — my Physical score went from 23 to 71.
That's not motivation. That's a system."

CTA (52–60s):
"Link in bio. It's free to start."
```

---

**App features to show:** Habit streaks → Badge unlock → Gamification (XP/levels) → Challenges

**Hook C** — Game reveal:
> "I didn't know a productivity app could make me feel like I was leveling up in an RPG"

**Hook D** — Challenge:
> "What's the longest streak you've ever had? Mine used to be 3 days."

---

[Screen recording: Habit check-in → XP animation → badge unlock celebration overlay →

## Screen Recording Cheat Sheet

**Most visually compelling screens to capture:**
1. Life Balance wheel (radar chart with obvious imbalance then improvement)
2. Badge unlock celebration overlay (dopamine hit on camera)
3. AI coach streaming response (typing effect = dynamic)
4. Journal Wizard AI follow-up questions (conversational, relatable)
5. Focus Timer ring animation + ambient sound toggle
6. Habit check-in → XP pop → streak counter
7. Retrospective calendar (proof of consistency)
8. Smart Goal Generator: plain text in → structured goal out

**Avoid showing:** Settings, sign-in, backup, any loading states

## What NOT to Do on TikTok

- Never open with "So I found this amazing app..." (ad brain activates, swipe)
- Never show a price or "download" button mid-video
- Never show more than 2 features per video
- Never use stock music that sounds like a corporate ad
- Never start with your face before the hook text is shown
- Never make it look too polished — authenticity > production quality
